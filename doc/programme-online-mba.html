What is an Online MBA?

An Online MBA is a 2-year postgraduate management programme delivered through scheduled live faculty sessions, recorded lectures, peer cohort forums, graded assignments, and proctored online examinations. Unlike a traditional Distance MBA where students study mostly self-paced from printed or digital materials, an Online MBA recreates the structure of an on-campus MBA in a fully remote format.

In India, leading private universities began offering proper Online MBA programmes around 2019 to 2020 after the UGC formally permitted online degree delivery. By 2026, more than 12 private deemed universities and 8 private universities offer UGC-DEB recognised Online MBA programmes that are formally equivalent to a regular MBA.

Is Online MBA right for you?

This mode suits you if

  • You're a working professional with 1 to 8 years of experience
  • You want a structured cohort experience close to a traditional MBA
  • You can commit to live sessions on evenings or weekends
  • You value live faculty interaction over self-paced flexibility
  • Your budget is ₹1.2L to ₹3L over 2 years (EMI available)

Look elsewhere if

  • You need full schedule flexibility, consider Distance MBA instead
  • You have 8+ years of experience and want a senior peer cohort, consider Executive MBA
  • Your budget is under ₹1L, Distance MBA options like LPU or IGNOU (govt) may suit better
  • You want a fully on-campus experience, consider a regular MBA from a B-school
